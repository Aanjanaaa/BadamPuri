Badam Puri Delights Recipe

Ingredients

  • Almond Flour - 500g
  • Granulated Sugar - 200g
  • Milk Powder - 50g
  • Milk - 6 tbsp (Additionally added 2 - 3 tbsp as required and adjust as needed)
  • Cardamom Powder - 1 tsp
  • Saffron - generous 2 pinch
  • Ghee - for greasing

Preparation Steps

  1. Microwave milk and saffron for 30 seconds. Allow to cool.
  2. Combine almond flour, sugar, milk powder, and cardamom powder in a bowl. Incorporate the saffron-infused milk to form a firm dough. Divide into three portions.
  3. Take this dough between parchment paper sheets and gently roll the dough with a rolling-pin. Roll out a portion of the dough to medium thickness. Use a grease cookie cutter for uniform puris and place on a tray.
  4. Preheat your oven and broil the puris for 4-5 minutes until a slight color change. Remove, cool slightly, then flip and broil again for 4-5 minutes until done.
  5. Allow the Badam Puris to cool. Serve and enjoy every bite.
